# Prompt Template: SOP / Policy Creation

Use this template when you need to create Standard Operating Procedures, policies, or process documentation.

## Template Structure

```
You are a [ROLE/SPECIALTY] creating a [DOCUMENT TYPE] for [ORGANIZATION].

CONTEXT:
- Organization: [Type, size, industry]
- Compliance requirements: [Relevant regulations - FCRA, EEOC, ADA, FMLA, etc.]
- Process scope: [When/how this applies]
- Current state: [What exists now, if anything]
- Stakeholders: [Who is involved - HR, managers, employees, etc.]

REQUIRED SECTIONS:
1. Purpose & Scope
   - What this [policy/SOP] covers
   - Who it applies to
   - When it applies

2. Definitions (if applicable)
   - Key terms that need clarification
   - Regulatory definitions

3. Roles & Responsibilities
   - [Role 1]: [Specific responsibilities]
   - [Role 2]: [Specific responsibilities]
   - [Role 3]: [Specific responsibilities]

4. Process Steps / Procedures
   - Step 1: [Action]
   - Step 2: [Action]
   - Step 3: [Action]
   [Continue with detailed steps]

5. Compliance Requirements
   - [Regulation 1]: [Specific requirements]
   - [Regulation 2]: [Specific requirements]
   - Documentation needed
   - Record retention

6. Special Considerations
   - Exceptions or unique scenarios
   - Position-specific requirements
   - State-specific variations (if applicable)

7. Documentation & Recordkeeping
   - What to document
   - Where to store
   - Retention period

8. Exception Handling
   - What to do when standard process doesn't apply
   - Escalation procedures
   - Approval requirements

9. Related Policies/Procedures
   - [Link to related policy 1]
   - [Link to related policy 2]

10. Review & Approval
    - Review schedule (annual, bi-annual)
    - Approval authority
    - Version control

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Professional [SOP/policy] format with numbered sections
- Include [decision trees / flowcharts / templates] for key processes
- Add compliance callout boxes for legal requirements
- Provide [email templates / forms / checklists] where applicable
- Maximum [X] pages, scannable with clear headers
- Include version control footer and approval signature block

TONE: [Professional / Compliance-focused / Operationally clear]
Assume audience has [basic / intermediate / advanced] knowledge.

DELIVERABLES:
- Main [SOP/policy] document
- [Supporting template 1]
- [Supporting template 2]
- Quick reference guide (1-page)
```

## Customization Guide

### Fill in these fields:

**[ROLE/SPECIALTY]:**
- HR compliance specialist
- Benefits administrator
- Talent acquisition specialist
- Employee relations manager
- Compensation analyst
- HRIS administrator

**[DOCUMENT TYPE]:**
- Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
- Policy document
- Process guide
- Compliance manual
- Procedure manual

**[ORGANIZATION]:**
- Your company name + type (e.g., "Creighton University, higher education")
- Size (small <50, medium 50-500, large 500-5000, enterprise 5000+)
- Industry context

**[COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS]:**
Choose relevant regulations:
- FCRA (Fair Credit Reporting Act) - background checks
- EEOC (Equal Employment Opportunity) - discrimination
- ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) - accommodations
- FMLA (Family Medical Leave Act) - leave
- FLSA (Fair Labor Standards Act) - wage/hour
- OFCCP (Federal Contract Compliance) - affirmative action
- HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability) - health information
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) - financial controls
- State-specific laws (ban-the-box, salary history bans, etc.)

**[TONE OPTIONS]:**
- Professional, compliance-focused, operationally clear (default for SOPs)
- Formal and authoritative (for policies)
- Instructional and supportive (for guides)
- Technical and precise (for system procedures)

## Tips for Best Results

✅ **Be specific about your organization**
- Include organization type, size, and industry
- Mention any unique cultural or mission considerations
- Note if you're in a highly regulated industry

✅ **List all applicable compliance requirements**
- Better to include too many than too few
- AI can filter what's relevant
- Ensures nothing critical is missed

✅ **Define stakeholders clearly**
- Who initiates the process?
- Who approves?
- Who executes?
- Who receives the outcome?

✅ **Request practical tools**
- Email templates for communication
- Decision trees for complex scenarios
- Checklists for compliance verification
- Forms for documentation

✅ **Set length constraints**
- SOPs: 3-5 pages typical
- Policies: 2-4 pages typical
- Quick reference: 1 page
- Adjust based on complexity

## Common Additions

### For Complex Processes:
Add to DELIVERABLES section:
- Process flowchart (visual representation)
- Decision tree for exception handling
- FAQ document for common questions
- Training presentation for rollout

### For Multi-Location Organizations:
Add to SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS section:
- State-specific compliance variations
- Location-specific procedures
- Regional approval authorities

### For Highly Regulated Industries:
Add to COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS section:
- Industry-specific regulations (healthcare, financial services, etc.)
- Audit trail requirements
- Third-party oversight considerations
