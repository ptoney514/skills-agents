**Development Coordinator**
Cristo Rey Jesuit Retreat Center
*Phoenix, Arizona*

Cristo Rey Jesuit Retreat Center, located in Phoenix, Arizona, is seeking a mission-driven Development Coordinator. This full-time position plays a vital role in supporting the Center's fundraising efforts and donor engagement initiatives, helping sustain our ministry of providing transformative retreat experiences to over 5,000 retreatants annually.

The Development Coordinator will work closely with the Director of Development to coordinate fundraising events, manage donor relationships, and support grant writing efforts. This is a relationship-building position rooted in Ignatian Spirituality, ideal for someone who seeks meaningful work in a faith-based environment and values being part of a collaborative team committed to spiritual formation.

Responsibilities include coordinating signature fundraising events (annual gala, golf tournament, appeal campaigns), managing the donor database using Raiser's Edge, crafting compelling donor communications and acknowledgment letters, supporting grant application processes, and contributing to the overall advancement strategy of the retreat center. The role requires some evening and weekend availability during events and participation in the Center's rhythm of prayer and community life.

The ideal candidate must be a Roman Catholic in good standing with 2-3 years of experience in fundraising, nonprofit work, or development. Strong writing skills are essential for donor communications. The position requires attention to detail for database management, collaborative spirit for working within a small team environment, and genuine alignment with the mission of Ignatian retreat ministry. Training or experience with Ignatian Spirituality, the Spiritual Exercises, and/or retreat ministry is strongly preferred. A Bachelor's degree is preferred.

Cristo Rey Jesuit Retreat Center sits on a beautiful 45-acre desert property and serves as a sanctuary for individuals and groups seeking spiritual renewal through the Ignatian tradition. Our small staff of 15 works together to create welcoming, transformative experiences for all who come through our doors.

Salary and benefits are commensurate with the position and experience, with a range of $42,000-$48,000 annually. Benefits include health insurance, dental coverage, and retirement matching. This is an on-site position based at our Phoenix location. Interested candidates should send a resume and cover letter via email to [email protected] by [deadline].
