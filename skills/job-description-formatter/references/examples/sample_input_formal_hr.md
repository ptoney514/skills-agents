## POSITION DESCRIPTION

**Position Title:** Associate Director of Student Engagement
**Department:** Student Life
**Reports To:** Dean of Students
**FLSA Status:** Exempt
**Grade:** 10
**Job Code:** SL-2024-003

### POSITION SUMMARY

The Associate Director of Student Engagement provides leadership and oversight for student activities, student organizations, and campus programming. This position serves as a key member of the Student Life leadership team and works collaboratively with faculty, staff, and students to create meaningful co-curricular experiences.

### ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS

**Student Organization Management (40%)**
- Oversee registration and recognition of 150+ student organizations
- Advise Student Government Association and Student Activities Board
- Review and approve student organization budgets totaling $500K annually
- Ensure compliance with university policies and procedures
- Manage student organization risk management protocols
- Coordinate leadership development training for organization officers

**Campus Programming (30%)**
- Plan and execute major campus events including Welcome Week, Homecoming, and Spring Fest
- Supervise Campus Activities Coordinator and 2 graduate assistants
- Manage annual programming budget of $200K
- Develop assessment strategies for all programs and events
- Collaborate with Residence Life on residential programming initiatives

**Student Development (20%)**
- Provide one-on-one advising to student leaders
- Facilitate leadership workshops and training sessions
- Mentor students through organizational challenges and conflicts
- Support diversity and inclusion initiatives within student organizations
- Foster connections between student life and academic affairs

**Administrative Responsibilities (10%)**
- Serve on university committees as assigned
- Maintain accurate records and documentation
- Prepare reports for senior leadership
- Participate in crisis response and on-call rotation
- Other duties as assigned

### MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS

**Education:**
- Master's degree in Higher Education, Student Affairs, or related field required

**Experience:**
- Minimum 3 years professional experience in student affairs
- Minimum 2 years supervising staff or student employees
- Demonstrated experience with budget management
- Experience advising student organizations

**Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:**
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills (written and verbal)
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite
- Knowledge of student development theory
- Ability to work flexible hours including evenings and weekends
- Valid driver's license

### PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

- 5+ years experience in higher education student affairs
- Experience at a religiously-affiliated institution
- Familiarity with Jesuit or Catholic higher education
- Experience with event management software (e.g., 25Live, Presence)
- Bilingual (Spanish preferred)

### PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS

- Ability to sit for extended periods
- Ability to lift up to 25 pounds occasionally
- Ability to stand and walk for extended periods during events
- Ability to work in various indoor and outdoor settings

### WORK ENVIRONMENT

- Standard office environment
- Regular evening and weekend work required
- Travel to conferences and off-campus events occasionally
- On-call rotation for student emergencies

### SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Campus Activities Coordinator (1 FTE)
- Graduate Assistants (2)
- Student employees (approximately 15)

### ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

- Salary Range: $55,000 - $65,000
- Position is benefits-eligible
- Expected start date: July 1, 2025
- Background check required
