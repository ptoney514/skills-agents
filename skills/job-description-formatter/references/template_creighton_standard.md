# Template 1: Creighton University Standard Format

This template is used for most Creighton University job postings. It provides a comprehensive, professional structure that emphasizes the opportunity, responsibilities, qualifications, and institutional context.

## Structure

### Header
```
**[Position Title]**
[Department/Unit Name]
*[Location]*
```

### Opening Paragraph
Start with: "Creighton University, a premier Jesuit Catholic institution ranked among the nation's top universities, seeks [descriptor] to serve as [position title]."

Include:
- Key reporting relationship
- Brief positioning statement about the role's importance

### The Opportunity Section
**Bold heading: "The Opportunity:"**

Describe:
- Comprehensive scope of the role
- Geographic/organizational reach (if applicable)
- Key value proposition for the candidate
- Strategic importance of the position

Follow with 1-2 additional paragraphs expanding on:
- Primary responsibilities
- Collaboration relationships
- Working environment details
- Schedule/flexibility notes (if applicable)

### Key Responsibilities Section
**Bold heading: "Key Responsibilities:"**

Use bullet points (•) for 5-10 key responsibilities:
- Start each with an action verb
- Be specific and outcome-oriented
- Include supervisory responsibilities if applicable
- Group related duties together

### Required Qualifications Section
**Bold heading: "Required Qualifications:"**

Use bullet points (•) listing:
- Education requirements
- Years of experience needed
- Certifications or licenses
- Essential skills or knowledge areas
- Specific technical competencies

### Preferred Qualifications Section
**Bold heading: "Preferred:"**

Use bullet points (•) listing:
- Advanced degrees
- Additional certifications
- Specialized experience
- Bonus skills

### Closing Statement
"Join a university community committed to academic excellence and service to others, with nearly $2 billion in assets and recognized for innovation and value in higher education."

### Application Information
"**[Application instructions and timeline]**" (bolded)

Include:
- Application deadline or review start date
- Start date information if known
- Salary information if appropriate

### About Creighton Section
**Bold heading: "About Creighton"**

Standard paragraph 1:
"At Creighton, we offer meaningful work driven by a 475-year-old Jesuit tradition within the broader context of Catholic education with a strong commitment to truth, service, and a faith that does justice. Through this tradition, we push the boundaries of innovation. We pursue new knowledge for the betterment of society. We carefully consider the ethical implications of new ideas and scientific advances. We seek to improve our world through the application of our Catholic and Jesuit values."

Standard paragraph 2 (location/rankings):
"The Omaha metro area is a diverse community of about one million residents with four Fortune-500 companies, an active sports scene, and a vibrant arts community. Recent Wall Street Journal/Times Higher Education College rankings recognized Creighton University among the top 20% of institutions in its survey and among the Top 10 big-city colleges in the Midwest. Creighton University also ranks in the top third of National Universities in U.S. News & World Report's and was previously recognized for 16 years in a row as No. 1 among Best Regional Universities – Midwest. In recognition of Creighton's excellence in and commitment to undergraduate research, Creighton is among the top Goldwater-producing Catholic universities."

## Tone Guidelines

- Professional and aspirational
- Emphasize collaboration and mission
- Use "you'll" for a more engaging tone in opportunity descriptions
- Balance specificity with inspiration
- Highlight both the practical role and strategic importance

## Key Phrases

- "seeks an experienced..." / "seeks a dynamic..." / "seeks a dedicated..."
- "This key leadership role..." / "This essential position..."
- "Working in collaboration with..."
- "comprehensive," "strategic," "essential"
- References to Jesuit values and mission when appropriate
