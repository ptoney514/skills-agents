# Template 2: Jesuit Ministry Standard Format

This template is used for positions at Jesuit retreat centers, communities, and ministries (not directly Creighton University positions, but often affiliated). This format is more concise and focused.

## Structure

### Header
```
**[Position Title]**
[Organization Name]
*[City, State]*
```

### Opening Paragraph
Start with the organization name and location, then briefly describe what they're seeking:

"[Organization Name], located in [City, State], is seeking [descriptor] [Position Title]."

Follow with 1-2 sentences about:
- The role's purpose or mission alignment
- Key responsibilities at a high level
- Required presence/schedule if notable

### Body Paragraphs (2-4 paragraphs)

Paragraph 1-2: Role details
- Expand on primary responsibilities
- Reporting relationships
- Key stakeholder interactions
- Required characteristics (Catholic in good standing, Ignatian Spirituality knowledge, etc.)

Paragraph 3: Qualifications
- Education requirements
- Experience needed
- Preferred qualifications
- Special requirements (e.g., work schedule, travel)

Paragraph 4 (if needed): Additional context
- Organizational context (e.g., "serves over 7,000 retreatants annually")
- Facility details if relevant
- Mission/vision alignment requirements

### Closing
"Salary and benefits are commensurate with the position and experience. [Additional application instructions]. Interested candidates should send a resume and cover letter [with references if required] via email to [email] by [deadline]."

Alternative: "Click **here** for the complete job description and to apply to the position."

### Posted Date
"*Posted [date]*"

## Tone Guidelines

- Warm and mission-focused
- Emphasize spiritual and relational aspects
- More concise than university postings
- Direct and clear about requirements
- Often mentions Catholic identity and Ignatian Spirituality explicitly

## Key Phrases

- "seeking a dynamic, people person with a desire for meaningful work"
- "rooted in Ignatian Spirituality"
- "candidate must be a Roman Catholic in good standing"
- "training or experience with Ignatian Spirituality, the Spiritual Exercises, and/or retreat ministry is preferred"
- "This is a relationship building position"
- "fosters the spiritual development"
- "commensurate with the position and experience"

## Common Elements

- Explicit Catholic identity requirements
- References to Ignatian Spirituality and mission
- Servant leadership language
- Relationship-building emphasis
- Often includes facility/site descriptions (e.g., "57-acre site")
- Work schedule specifics (e.g., "four days/week, with some evening and weekend work")
